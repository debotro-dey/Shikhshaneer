    Welcome to Shikhshaneer

    Shikhshaneer is an esteemed educational initiative dedicated to fostering academic excellence through personalized guidance and a supportive learning environment. Rooted in the belief that individual attention is key to holistic development, Shikhshaneer offers a homely atmosphere where students are nurtured intellectually, emotionally, and ethically. With a strong emphasis on discipline, quality teaching, and moral values, the institution aims to cultivate well-rounded individuals equipped to excel in both academic pursuits and future endeavors.

    Science & Commerce
    Department

    Arkajit Dasgupta (Founder/Principal)

    The Science & Commerce Department of Shikhshaneer is a cornerstone of academic excellence, fostering curiosity, innovation, and critical thinking. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Arkajit Dasgupta, the department has evolved into a thriving hub where students receive personalized guidance and mentorship in both scientific and commercial disciplines.

    Our Science stream provides students with a strong foundation in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer Science—preparing them for competitive examinations, research pathways, and professional courses. Simultaneously, the Commerce stream cultivates future business leaders through comprehensive studies in Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, Legal Studies, and Entrepreneurship.

    The department's holistic teaching methodology balances academic rigor with practical exposure, ensuring that students not only score well but also understand real-world applications. With regular interactive sessions, project-based learning, and close faculty-student interaction, our aim is to nurture confident, capable individuals who are ready to contribute meaningfully to society.

    Mr. Dasgupta’s dedication to both excellence and empathy ensures every student feels inspired and supported throughout their academic journey.

    Humanities
    Department

    Rimi Majumder (Founder/Principal)

    The Humanities Department at Shikhshaneer is dedicated to nurturing creativity, empathy, and a deep understanding of society and culture. Led by Ms. Rimi Majumder, an inspiring educator with a passion for human values and critical thinking, the department provides a comprehensive learning experience in subjects such as English, History, Geography, Political Science, Sociology, Education, Hindi, and Bengali.

    Ms. Majumder believes in empowering students to explore ideas, question perspectives, and appreciate the diversity of human experiences. Her approach blends academic rigor with interactive discussions, research projects, and cultural activities that encourage students to connect classroom learning with the real world.

    With a supportive and inclusive environment, the Humanities Department ensures that every student develops strong analytical skills, emotional intelligence, and the confidence to become a thoughtful contributor to society.
